The excitement is palpable as beXide announces the full release of *Fruit Mountain Party* on April 23rd, marking a significant milestone for this anime-styled 3D puzzle game. After a successful early access phase, where player feedback played a crucial role, the developers are set to deliver an enhanced gameplay experience on PC via Steam and Nintendo Switch platforms.

*Fruit Mountain Party* takes the beloved mechanics of merging fruits and transforms it into a chaotic party battle game, allowing up to four players to compete locally or online. With various game modes like Standard Race and Thief Frenzy, players can enjoy a diverse range of challenges while honing their skills against NPCs in single-player mode. The games vibrant graphics and engaging mechanics are sure to keep players entertained for hours.

As we anticipate the full release, it's clear that *Fruit Mountain Party* is poised to become a favorite among puzzle game enthusiasts. Will you be ready to stack your fruits and take on friends in this delightful battle? Let's find out on launch day!
