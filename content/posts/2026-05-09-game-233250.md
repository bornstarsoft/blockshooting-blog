In a bold move that could reshape the gaming landscape, Arjan Brussee, co-founder of Guerilla Games and former technical director at Epic Games, is developing a new game engine called 'The Immense Engine'. This initiative aims to provide a fully European alternative to dominant American and Chinese engines like Unreal and Unity, emphasizing compliance with European regulations and guidelines.

Brussee's vision is not just about gaming; he sees potential applications for 3D simulations in various sectors, including defense and logistics. He believes that the rise of AI necessitates a new approach to game engine development, one that integrates AI capabilities to enhance productivity and creativity. This could mean that a small team, equipped with the right AI tools, could achieve what traditionally required a much larger workforce.

As the gaming industry continues to evolve, the introduction of The Immense Engine could pave the way for a more diverse and innovative ecosystem. Will this European alternative succeed in challenging the established giants of the industry? Only time will tell.
