Get ready for a wild ride as the highly anticipated urban survival game, "Good Luck," is set to launch on PC on April 8, 2026. Developed by the innovative Turkish studio Happybara Games, this physics-based platformer promises to deliver a unique blend of chaotic gameplay and lighthearted humor. Players will navigate a city filled with absurd traps and unexpected hazards, all while racing to reach a meeting. With both single-player and online co-op modes for up to five players, the game is designed to keep you on your toes.

In "Good Luck," the unpredictability of urban life takes center stage. Forget about checkpoints; every jump, climb, and dodge could lead to hilarious mishaps or epic victories. The game's mechanics draw inspiration from the rage game subgenre, ensuring that players experience both frustration and joy as they tackle the challenges ahead. Whether youre playing solo or teaming up with friends, the chaotic physics and whimsical dangers will make every session memorable.

As we count down to the release, one can't help but wonder: how will players adapt to the madness of the urban jungle? With its unique approach to survival and co-op gameplay, "Good Luck" is set to redefine the genre and offer a fresh experience for gamers everywhere.
