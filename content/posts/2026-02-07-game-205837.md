In a significant move to enhance its global gaming ecosystem, JetSynthesys has acquired the top-grossing merge puzzle game EverMerge, which has amassed over $350 million in lifetime revenue. This acquisition not only underscores JetSynthesys' ambition to expand its reach across multiple genres and markets but also highlights the game's strong performance in high-growth regions like the US and Western Europe. With EverMerge ranked among the top 100 highest-grossing mobile games of all time, the potential for further growth is immense.

Rajan Navani, Chairman & MD of JetSynthesys, emphasized the importance of this acquisition in bridging global intellectual properties with local insights, especially in emerging markets like India. The company plans to leverage its expertise in community engagement and user acquisition to resonate with new audiences through innovative storytelling and technology. As JetSynthesys continues to invest in EverMerge, the gaming community can expect fresh content and engaging experiences that keep the gameplay evolving.

This acquisition marks a pivotal moment for JetSynthesys as it aims to solidify its position as a leading global games company. With the gaming industry constantly evolving, how will JetSynthesys shape the future of mobile gaming in diverse markets?
