SEGA is set to elevate the gaming experience for fans of Persona5: The Phantom X with the highly anticipated update 2.7, which introduces beloved characters Justine and Caroline from the original Persona 5. This update, launching on December 25, marks the game's half-anniversary and promises to enrich the narrative and gameplay with these iconic figures.

As a free-to-play gacha game, Persona5: The Phantom X offers a fresh perspective on the Persona universe, allowing players to engage with a new cast of Phantom Thieves. The addition of Justine and Caroline, known for their roles as the Velvet Room attendants, is particularly exciting as they bring unique abilities and strategies to the game. This simultaneous worldwide reveal is a significant milestone, showcasing SEGA's commitment to its global player base.

In addition to the character update, players can look forward to daily log-in bonuses leading up to the anniversary, as well as a collaboration with DARTSLIVE that introduces themed cosmetic items. With these developments, the future looks bright for Persona5: The Phantom X. Are you ready to join the new Phantom Thieves and explore what they have to offer?
