The upcoming book "Portal. Cube, Cake, and Artificial Intelligence" offers a fascinating glimpse into the evolution of one of gaming's most iconic titles. From a bold student project to a cult classic, this narrative showcases how innovative ideas can transform the gaming landscape. With insights into the principles of design and player engagement, readers will discover why the game remains relevant and beloved.

Published by Bombora, this book not only chronicles the history of Portal but also delves into the psychological aspects of gaming, such as the principle of "less is more" and the importance of a relaxed state for learning. The exploration of GLaDOS and the iconic song "Still Alive" adds layers to the understanding of game mechanics and storytelling.

As we await the book's release in May 2026, it raises an intriguing question: How will the lessons learned from Portal continue to influence future game development?
