In an exciting turn of events for gamers, Nintendo's latest titles, Mario Kart World and Donkey Kong Bananza, have both been shortlisted for the prestigious 'Console Game of the Year' at the Golden Joystick Awards. This recognition not only highlights the innovative gameplay and design of these titles but also marks a significant moment for the newly launched Switch 2, which is quickly establishing itself as a formidable player in the gaming industry.

With Mario Kart World competing for 'Best Multiplayer' and Donkey Kong Bananza nominated for 'Best Audio Design', fans are eagerly anticipating the results. The competition is fierce, with heavyweights like Death Stranding 2 and Ghost of Ytei also in the running. As the gaming community votes for their favorites, the anticipation builds for the awards ceremony on November 21, 2025.

As we look forward to the announcement of the winners, one can't help but wonder: will Nintendo's latest offerings take home the coveted titles, or will they face stiff competition from other innovative games? The excitement is palpable, and the gaming world is watching closely.
