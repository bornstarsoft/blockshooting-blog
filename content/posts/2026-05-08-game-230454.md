Capcom has just dropped a thrilling new mode for Resident Evil Requiem, titled "Leon Must Die Forever," which is now available as a free DLC. This fast-paced mini-game is unlocked upon completing the main story, offering players a fresh challenge with numerous stages to conquer as Leon, all while unlocking exclusive enhancer abilities.

The new mode promises to deliver a distinct gameplay experience compared to the main story, packed with replay value and multiple difficulty levels, including an extreme mode for the bravest players. As the name suggests, players will face the ultimate test of survival, with the stakes higher than ever. Can you navigate through the challenges and defeat the boss within the time limit?
