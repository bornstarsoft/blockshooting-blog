Exciting news for gamers! The beloved 3D puzzle game, Fruit Mountain Party, is making its way to the Switch 2 and Switch in spring 2026. Originally launched in Early Access for PC, this multiplayer party game has captured the hearts of players with its unique fruit-combining mechanics and engaging gameplay. The announcement was made by developer BeXide, and fans are eagerly anticipating the chance to stack fruits and compete in vibrant battles on their consoles.

For those who can't wait, the Switch version will be showcased at the Osaka Game Dungeon on December 27 and the Tokyo eSports Festa from January 9 to January 11, 2026. Players can expect a variety of modes, including heated online battles and a single-player ranking system that promises to keep the competition fierce. With its easy-to-learn mechanics and chaotic fun, Fruit Mountain Party is set to be a hit on the Switch platforms.
