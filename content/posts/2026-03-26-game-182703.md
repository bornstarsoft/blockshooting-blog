In an exciting development for retro gaming enthusiasts, Hyperkin has teamed up with ModRetro to unveil a new line of color-matched controllers for the upcoming M64 gaming console. This collaboration promises to elevate the gaming experience with the Captain Premium Controllers, known for their durability and comfort, designed to withstand even the most intense gaming sessions. With a generous 10-foot cable and an extension port for memory cards and rumble feedback, these controllers are set to enhance gameplay like never before.

The new Captain Premium Controllers will be available in vibrant colors such as Green, Clear, Purple, and Red, alongside existing color options. This variety not only adds a personal touch for gamers but also reflects the nostalgia of the classic N64 era. As Slade Dude Suzuki, Lead Product Developer at Hyperkin, expressed, this partnership is a dream come true for retro gaming fans, merging beloved hardware with innovative design.

As we look forward to the release of these controllers later this year, one can't help but wonder: how will this collaboration redefine the retro gaming landscape?
