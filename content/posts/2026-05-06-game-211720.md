The conversation around video game actors is gaining momentum, with Alix Wilton Regan, the new voice of Lara Croft, emphasizing that these performers deserve recognition for their hard work and dedication. In a recent interview, she highlighted the physical and emotional demands of video game acting, stating that it is filled with "blood and sweat and tears," especially during performance capture sessions.

Regan also praised the cast of Baldurs Gate 3 for inspiring other actors to acknowledge their contributions to the gaming experience. She believes that the industry is beginning to realize the value of these performances, which elevate the overall storytelling and gameplay. As the landscape of video games continues to evolve, its crucial that we celebrate the talent behind the characters we love.

As we look forward to the upcoming Tomb Raider games, its clear that the role of voice actors and motion capture artists is more important than ever. Will we see more recognition for these artists in the future? Only time will tell.
