Get ready to embark on a whimsical journey with "Quack Quack Attack - PEGG Blaster," a new mobile puzzle game that promises to captivate players with its unique gameplay mechanics and charming storyline. Players will take on the role of Captain Quack, leading a team of avian allies on a quest to reclaim their oceanic home from monstrous foes. The game combines strategy and action in a pachinko-style format, where players launch eggs to defeat enemies and clear stages.

With over fifty unique birds to choose from, players can experiment with different class combinations to optimize their strategies. The game not only offers a rich main campaign but also features various modes like the Purgatory Tower for skill honing and the Starry Expedition for competitive play. Plus, pre-registration is now open, offering exciting rewards that enhance the gaming experience right from launch.
