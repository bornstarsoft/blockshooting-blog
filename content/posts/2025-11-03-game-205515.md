If you're a fan of word games, you'll want to check out Shuffalo, the latest daily puzzle from The New Yorker. This engaging game challenges players to spell words from scrambled letters, and with each correct answer, a new letter is added to the mix. It's not just about finding words; it's about adapting to the increasing complexity, making it a fun and stimulating experience for word enthusiasts.

Shuffalo is available on The New Yorkers Puzzles and Games page, providing a fresh daily challenge that keeps players coming back for more. Whether you're looking to sharpen your vocabulary or just enjoy a quick brain workout, this game offers a perfect blend of fun and mental exercise. As the game evolves with each round, it invites players to think creatively and strategically.
