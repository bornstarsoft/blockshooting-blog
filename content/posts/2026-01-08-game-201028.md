The newly launched game AiliA invites players into a captivating world where reality and reflection intertwine, offering a unique mirror-based puzzle experience. Developed by IceToad Studio LLC and published by Gamirror Games, AiliA is now available on Steam for $11.99, with a 20% discount for early adopters. This game stands out not just for its engaging puzzles but also for its innovative use of mirrors as interactive elements that transform gameplay.

In AiliA, players take on the role of Aria, navigating through beautifully crafted stages that blend the real and mirrored worlds. The puzzles are designed to challenge players' perception and problem-solving skills, utilizing water reflections and recursive mirror structures. With over 100 puzzles spread across seven distinct areas, players can also uncover hidden stages, making each playthrough a fresh adventure.

For those intrigued by the concept, a free demo is available on Steam, allowing players to dive into this enchanting world. As the gaming landscape evolves, AiliA sets a new standard for puzzle games, inviting us to ponder: how will the integration of innovative mechanics like these shape the future of interactive storytelling in gaming?
