Get ready for an exciting journey into the dream world of "HER TREES: PUZZLE DREAM," a point-and-click puzzle game set to launch on February 10, 2026. Developed by solo creator stone, this game invites players to engage with a unique puzzle system where the solution emerges through the creative rearrangement of objects like scraps of paper and leaves. Unlike traditional puzzle games, the objects in this game can be freely placed via drag-and-drop, encouraging players to think outside the box and discover surprising solutions.
