This decision not only set the tone for the game but also highlighted the importance of adapting strategies under pressure.

Despite a strong performance from Jaylen Brown and Derrick White, who combined for 59 points, the Celtics' early deficit proved insurmountable. The game underscores the necessity for teams to make calculated decisions, especially when star players are sidelined. As the Celtics look to regroup for next season, questions linger about Mazzulla's approach and how it will evolve moving forward. Can the Celtics learn from this experience to avoid similar pitfalls in the future?
